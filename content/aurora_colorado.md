# Aurora, Colorado

## Crime and Public Safety

### High-profile incidents and investigations

On July 20, 2012, James Eagan Holmes, a 24-year-old former neuroscience graduate student, carried out a mass shooting at the Century 16 movie theater in Aurora during a midnight screening of The Dark Knight Rises, killing 12 people and injuring 70 others with firearms including a semi-automatic rifle, shotgun, and handgun.[129] Holmes had rigged his apartment with explosives, which were later neutralized by authorities, and surrendered to police outside the theater after the attack.[129] The investigation revealed Holmes had planned the attack for months, acquiring weapons legally, and suffered from mental health issues including schizophrenia, though he was deemed legally sane; he was convicted on 165 counts of murder, attempted murder, and explosives possession in July 2015 and sentenced to 12 consecutive life terms without parole. *See main article: [2012 Aurora theater shooting](https://grokipedia.com/page/2012_Aurora_shooting)*
